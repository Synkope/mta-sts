MTA-STS Policy for Synkope

This site hosts the MTA-STS (Mail Transfer Agent Strict Transport Security) policy for synkope.io.

Policy Location

The policy is available at: /.well-known/mta-sts.txt

What is MTA-STS?

MTA-STS is a security standard (RFC 8461) that helps prevent man-in-the-middle attacks on email delivery by requiring TLS encryption for mail delivery.
